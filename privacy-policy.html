2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect various types of information to provide, maintain, improve, and protect our Services. The information we collect falls into the following categories:

2.1 Personal Identification Information

Information that can be used to identify you individually:

  • Mobile Phone Number: Primary identifier for account creation and verification (mandatory);
  • Full Name: For account personalization and KYC purposes (mandatory for withdrawals);
  • Email Address: For communications and account recovery (optional);
  • Date of Birth: For age verification and compliance purposes;
  • Government-Issued ID: For KYC verification when withdrawing cashback (as required by law);

2.2 Financial and Transaction Information

  • Bank Account Details: For cashback withdrawal processing (account number, IFSC code, account holder name);
  • Payment Method Information: Partial payment information (last 4 digits of cards, UPI ID) - full payment details are processed by third-party payment gateways and not stored by us;
  • Transaction History: Complete record of all recharge transactions, amounts, dates, mobile numbers recharged, and transaction statuses;
  • Cashback Information: Cashback earned, used, withdrawn, and wallet balance;
  • Refund Information: Refund requests, approvals, rejections, and processing status;

2.3 Device and Technical Information

Information automatically collected when you use the App:

  • Device Information: Device model, manufacturer, operating system version, unique device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, Advertising ID);
  • App Usage Data: Features accessed, screens viewed, time spent on app, interaction patterns, frequency of use;
  • Location Information: Approximate location based on IP address (precise location only if you grant permission);
  • Network Information: Mobile carrier, network type (Wi-Fi, 4G, 5G), IP address, network status;
  • Log Data: Crash reports, error logs, diagnostic information, performance data, timestamps;
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Session tokens, authentication tokens, preferences;

2.4 Communications and Support Data

  • Customer Support Communications: Chat messages, emails, phone call recordings (with notice), support tickets, complaints, feedback;
  • Survey Responses: Feedback, ratings, reviews, suggestions;
  • Marketing Communications: Preferences for promotional communications;

2.5 Social Media and Third-Party Information

  • If you choose to link social media accounts, we may collect profile information such as name, email, profile picture;
  • Information from third-party services you choose to connect with the App;

2.6 Inferred and Derived Information

  • User Preferences: Preferred recharge amounts, frequently recharged numbers, operator preferences;
  • Behavioral Patterns: Usage patterns, transaction patterns, engagement metrics;
  • Risk Assessment Data: Fraud detection signals, security risk scores;

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication for sensitive operations;

7. DATA RETENTION

7.1 Retention Period: We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

7.2 Specific Retention Periods:

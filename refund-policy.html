Refund Policy

Our commitment to fair and transparent refund processing

Last Updated: December 2024
Important: This refund policy applies to all transactions made through Laddoo App. By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are processed in the following scenarios:

Automatic Refunds

  • Failed Recharge Transactions: If your recharge fails due to technical issues or network problems, the amount will be automatically refunded to your original payment method within 3-5 business days.
  • Duplicate Transactions: If you are charged twice for the same transaction, the duplicate amount will be automatically refunded upon verification.
  • System Errors: If there are technical errors on our part that prevent successful recharge, the amount will be refunded automatically.

Manual Refund Requests

  • Wrong Number Recharge: If you accidentally recharge the wrong number, you may request a refund within 24 hours of the transaction.
  • Incorrect Amount: If you recharge an incorrect amount due to input error, you may request a refund within 24 hours.
  • Service Issues: If you experience service-related issues that prevent you from using the recharged amount, you may be eligible for a refund.

2. Refund Process

1

Automatic Processing

For failed transactions, refunds are processed automatically without any action required from you. You will receive a notification once the refund is initiated.

2

Manual Request

For manual refunds, contact our support team through the app or email with your transaction details. Include the transaction ID and reason for the refund request.

3

Verification

Our team will verify the transaction and refund request. This process typically takes 1-2 business days.

4

Processing

Once approved, the refund will be processed to your original payment method within 3-5 business days.

3. Refund Timeline

  • Automatic Refunds: 3-5 business days from transaction date
  • Manual Refund Requests: 5-7 business days from approval
  • Bank Processing: Additional 1-3 business days depending on your bank

4. Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds will not be processed in the following cases:

  • Successful recharge transactions (amount credited to mobile number)
  • User error in entering mobile number (after 24 hours)
  • Refund requests made after 7 days of transaction date
  • Transactions where the user has already received cashback
  • Fraudulent or suspicious transactions
  • Refund requests without valid transaction proof

5. Cashback and Refunds

Important points regarding cashback and refunds:

  • If a refund is processed, any cashback earned on that transaction will be reversed
  • Cashback already used for other transactions will be deducted from the refund amount
  • If cashback has been withdrawn, it will be deducted from the refund amount

6. Refund Methods

Refunds are processed using the same payment method used for the original transaction:

  • UPI: Refunded to the same UPI ID
  • Credit/Debit Cards: Refunded to the same card
  • Net Banking: Refunded to the same bank account
  • Wallet: Refunded to the same wallet

7. Processing Fees

Refund processing is free of charge. However:

  • No processing fees are charged by Laddoo App
  • Bank or payment gateway charges (if any) are borne by the user
  • Currency conversion charges (for international cards) are not refunded

8. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision:

  • Contact our support team with detailed documentation
  • Provide transaction proof and evidence supporting your claim
  • Our team will review the case within 3-5 business days
  • Final decisions are made by our management team

9. Refund Status Tracking

You can track your refund status:

  • Through the Laddoo App under "Transaction History"
  • By contacting our support team
  • Through email notifications

10. Special Circumstances

10.1 Festival and Promotional Periods

During special promotional periods, refund policies remain the same, but processing times may be slightly longer due to high transaction volumes.

10.2 Technical Maintenance

During scheduled maintenance periods, refund processing may be delayed. Users will be notified in advance of any maintenance that affects refund processing.

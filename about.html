About Laddoo App

Revolutionizing mobile recharge with assured cashback for every user

Our Story

Laddoo App was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make mobile recharging not just convenient, but rewarding for every user. Founded in 2024, we recognized that mobile recharge is an essential service that millions of Indians use daily, yet most platforms only provide the basic service without any added value.

We set out to change this by creating a platform that not only offers seamless mobile recharge across all major telecom operators but also rewards users with assured cashback on every successful transaction. Our name "Laddoo" reflects our commitment to making every recharge feel like a sweet reward.

Today, Laddoo App has become a trusted name in the mobile recharge space, serving thousands of users across India with our innovative approach to customer satisfaction and value creation.

Our Mission

To democratize mobile recharge services by providing a platform that not only meets the basic need of recharging but also adds tangible value through assured cashback, making every transaction rewarding for our users.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and rewarding mobile recharge platform, where users don't just recharge their phones but also earn rewards that make their digital lives more fulfilling.

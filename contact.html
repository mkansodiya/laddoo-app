Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get cashback on my recharge?

You receive a scratch card on every successful recharge. Guaranteed cashback is credited instantly to your Laddoo wallet when you scratch the card. You can use this cashback for future recharges or withdraw it to your bank account.

What if my recharge fails?

If your recharge fails, the amount will be automatically refunded to your original payment method within 3-5 business days. You can also contact our support team for immediate assistance.

How secure is my payment information?

We use bank-grade encryption and secure payment gateways to protect your financial information. Your payment details are never stored on our servers and are processed through certified payment partners.

Can I recharge any mobile number?

Yes, you can recharge any valid Indian mobile number across all major telecom operators including Airtel, Jio, Vi, BSNL, and others.

How do I withdraw my cashback?

You can withdraw your cashback to your bank account through the app. The process usually takes 1-2 business days.

Is there a limit on recharge amounts?

You can recharge any amount from ₹10 to ₹10,000 per transaction. There's no daily limit on the number of recharges you can perform.
